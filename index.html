About Me

I am a nerd who thinks he is cool, says my wife. I love to cycle long distances on my bike with my family or friends, despite, having been hit by a car and having to learn how to walk again. My proudest moment was completing the MS 150 tour for cure. This was a two-day bike ride that was broken up into 75 miles per day. During the last 25 miles I felt like I had an out-of-body experience, since I was so tired and fatigued that I could not feel my body anymore thus feeling like I was physically not there. Being from the south, I enjoy cooking all types of Cajun foods such as gumbo, red beans & rice, and shrimp etouffee. The southern experience has definitely played a part in who I am today, and I am blessed to have had that experience.
